Pack Description
Retextures of assorted items in Hoxton Breakout:
The Director's screen, the servers (shared with other maps), the huge screens in day 2, ticket dispenser, ticket booths.

Updates
07/13/16 Added a re-texture for the DNA scanner screen. Now it simple and clear to understand.

Notes
I am now using lzma2 compression in .7z files.

How to Install
Please use 7-zip or WinRAR to open the .7z file and place the folders in
"C:\Program Files (x86)\Steam\SteamApps\common\PAYDAY 2\assets\mod_overrides"
